I'm always chasing the next interesting problem — whether it's on a chart, a racetrack, or under the hood.

I enjoy spending time on technical analysis and trading, but when I'm not staring at charts, I'm usually playing guitar.  My favorite getaway is relaxing with my wife at the lake, but most of the time you'll find me working on cars and diesel trucks.  Although I don't do it as much as I used to, I still love to race my Spec Miata or spend the weekend at great road course track day — there's nothing quite like it. 

Driven by an entrepreneurial mindset, I explore how AI can be leveraged to streamline business models and build high-impact solutions. I’m equally passionate about the EV sector, actively tracking emerging battery technologies that will disrupt the future of mobility.
